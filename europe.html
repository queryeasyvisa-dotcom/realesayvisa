Complete guide for Pakistani citizens applying for an Europe all Countrys Visit Visa

Europe All Schengen Countries Visa Requirements

Documents required for Europe visa application

  • Two application forms, completed and signed by applicant
  • Original Passport & Previous Passport (signature required on valid passport)
  • C.N.I.C Copy
  • Working and leave certificate (NOC)
  • Kids B-Form Copy (if children included)
  • Student I.D Card & complete school/college/university details (if child applicant)
  • 2 White Background photographs (35x45mm, matt finished)
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months (Personal)
  • Proof of income (Salary Certificate)
  • Business applicants: NTN / Tax Return for last 3 years
  • Applicant Visiting Card
  • Company letterhead request letter
  • Applicant Email Address & Phone Number (Res./Cell)
  • Marriage Certificate (English / NADRA)
  • Family Registration Certificate (FRC)
  • For employees: Last 3 months salary slip
  • Travel Insurance
  • Proof of sufficient financial means (travellers cheques, cash, credit cards)
  • Confirmed Hotel Reservation
  • Ticket / Flight Reservation
  • Travel Plan
  • Visa Fee: 80 Euro (Paid by Client)
  • File Process Fee: 65,000 PKR
