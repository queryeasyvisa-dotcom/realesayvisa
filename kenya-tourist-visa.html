Complete guide for Pakistani citizens applying for kenya Visitor Visa

Apply Now

Kenya Visitor Visa Requirements

  • Covering Letter
  • 2 recent passport size photographs
  • Photocopy of Passport
  • Copy of CNIC
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months
  • Copy of NTN Certificate
  • Hotel Reservation (1 copy to be faxed directly to the Embassy)
  • Visa Fees: Rs. 3000/-
  • Service Charges: Rs. 1000/-
  • Pay order through the Post Office (charges Rs. 100/-)
Processing Time: 2 Days (Tourist Visa)
Embassy Submission & Delivery Days & Time: Mon to Thurs: 09 to 11 a.m.

Kenya Business Visa Requirements

  • Covering Letter of the firm
  • Invitation Letter (Compulsory, 1 copy to be faxed directly to the Embassy)
  • 2 recent passport size photographs
  • Photocopy of Passport
  • Copy of CNIC
  • Bank Statement of the last 6 months
  • Copy of NTN Certificate
  • Letter from Chamber of Commerce (Only for Business)
  • Hotel Reservation (1 copy to be faxed directly to the Embassy)
  • Visa Fees: Rs. 3000/-
  • Service Charges: Rs. 1000/-
  • Pay order through the Post Office (charges Rs. 100/-)
Processing Time: 2 Days (Business Visa)
Embassy Submission & Delivery Days & Time: Mon to Thurs: 09 to 11 a.m.
