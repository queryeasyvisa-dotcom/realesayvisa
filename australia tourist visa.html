Australia Visitor Visa Overview

The Australia Visitor Visa (subclass 600) allows Pakistani citizens to visit Australia for tourism purposes, to see family and friends, or for other short-term non-work purposes. The visa is typically granted for stays of up to 3, 6, or 12 months.

Processing times for Australian tourist visas can vary from 2 weeks to 3 months depending on the completeness of your application and current workload at the embassy.

Visitor Visa (Subclass 600)

The Visitor visa (subclass 600) is for people who want to travel to Australia as tourists, for business or to visit family. It is a temporary visa.

Requirements:
  • Original Passport (6 months valid)
  • Original ID card
  • 4 Passport Photos (Blue Background)
  • Visa Request Letter
  • Normal Visa Fee 25 USD + Service Fee 5000 PKR
  • Processing Time: 3 Working Days
  • Urgent Visa Fee: 70 USD (Processing: 3 Hours)
  • File Making Fee: 65,000 PKR (advance)
